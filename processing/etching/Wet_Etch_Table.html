Notation: - = test not performed; W = Not performed, but known to Work ( 100 A/min); F = not performed, but known to be Fast ( 10 kA/min); P = some of film Peeled during etch or when rinsed; A = film was visibly Attached and roughened.

Etch areas are all of a 4-inch wafer for the transparent films and half of the wafer for single-crystal silicon and the metals.

Etch rates will vary with temperature and prior use of solution, area of exposure of film, other materials present (e.g., photoresist), film impurities and microstructure, etc. Some variation should be expected.
