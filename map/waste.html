Waste Containers
  • Pipette jug
    Disposal when full:
    • Obtain an empty (reuse) 1 gallon container from bottom wire shelf to the left of door from gowning area,
    • find a hazardous waste label on top of the acid cabinet and photoresist and MF319 labels on the side of acid cabinet
    • Copy contents from waste label on old container to new label and afix labels on new container
    • Place old container in secondary containment bin in gowning room, next to outside door (next to cardboard sharps box)
    • Contact Dartmouth EHS (email to ehs@dartmouth.edu)and ask for pickup in Room C223 gowning area.
  • Photoresist saturated wipes and aluminum foil
    Disposal when full: transfer to large waste bin on bottom wire shelf just outside litho room door.
