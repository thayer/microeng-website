Growth temperature/ growth rate

The Tradeoff:
  • Low temperature/ high rate: Less reaction products incorporated. Less misorientation in growing film.
  • High temperature/ low rate: Good lattice formation. More autodoping.
  • Overall trend is towards high rate/ low temperature processes.
A possible solution is to grow at a fast rate until the surface is completely covered, then at a slower rate to reach the desired thickness. Rate is more easily changed than temperature.
