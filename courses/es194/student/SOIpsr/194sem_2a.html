Substrate Materials

The three key factors which have been used to qualify the suitability of substrate materials for heteroepitaxial silicon-on-insulator are the following:
  • Lattice match between substrate and silicon
  • Contamination of silicon layer from substrate
  • Thermal contraction of substrate relative to silicon
  • Compatibility with existing silicon processing methods
(Cullen, 9)

The materials which have been found most suitable to this purpose include:

  • Sapphire (Al2O3)
  • Spinel (MgAl2O4)
