Magnetron Sputtering

Magnetron Sputtering use a megnetically enhanced cathode or "Magnetron", it has considerably expanded the potential os sputtering

  • Deposition pressure : 10^-3 to 0.1 Pa ( 10^-5-10^-3 torr)
  • Deposition rate : 0.2 to 2 E-6 m/min
  • Depostion temperature : 100 to 150 C
  • Comments:
    • Low deposition pressure, very high deposition rates, low substrate temperature, can be scaled up and commonly used for industrial production
    • more complex than diode systems
