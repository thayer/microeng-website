Diode Sputtering

DC/RF Diode Sputtering

  • Deposition pressure : 0.5 to 10 Pa ( 5*10^-3 to 0.1 torr)
  • Deposition rate : 0.02 to 0.2 E-6 m/min
  • Depostion temperature : 100 to 300 C
  • Comments:
    • Simple, relative ease in fabrication and thickness uniformity over large area
    • Realtively high deposition pressure and relatively high substrate temperature

  • Radio-Frequency Sputtering

    • Why use RF sputtering? a simple, effective, all-purpose sputtering system.
      • can sputter nonconductive materials.METHOD: P123
    • Only additional need for sputtering conductors is for a blocking capacitor to prevent shorting the target to ground through the matching network.
    • a typical RF sputtering system FIG.
    • Compare with DC glow discharge system, the RF system is :
      • capable of relatively high discharge system.
      • capable of relatively high coating rates.
      • can deposit both conductive materials and insulating materials
