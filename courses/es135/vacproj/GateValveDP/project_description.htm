Vacuum System Project

 

Gate Valve and Diffusion Pump

 

Igor  Paprotny & Trey Sears ?

 

 

Our part of the project was to disassemble, verify and grease o-rings, then assemble the gate valve and diffusion pump. We also verified the functionality of the cold trap, situated above the diffusion pump.

 

The gate valve consists of a circular plate riding in on rollers in a grove, pushed by the threaded valve rod, connecting the plate with the valve handle itself. There are three seals in the gate valve, a) the main gate valve seal, b) an o-ring sealing the rectangular plate of the gate valve cover, and c) the grease-seal around the gate-valve rod. A schematic of the system is presented in fig. 1.

We also investigated the cold trap, which is located above the diffusion pump. The insides of the cold-trap was non-standard, as shown on fig. 10.

 

 

Fig. 10. A peek into the cold-trap after the pump has been removed

 

We also tested the cold traps ability to actually, well, be cold ! Together with prof. Gibson we filled the cold trap with liquid nitrogen. As you can see (figs 11-13) , the insides got very cold. We even observed oxygen condensing and falling of as drops to the ground !
