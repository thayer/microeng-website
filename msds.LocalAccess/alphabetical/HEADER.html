Materials Safety Data Sheets

The materials safety data sheet (MSDS) database indexed alphabetically below provides information needed to handle these chemicals safely.

Only materials used by this lab are included in this list. This reduced database makes it easier to find the appropriate information. Nevertheless, it is incombant on the user to verify that the form and concentration listed in the MSDS choosen corresponds to that of the material in use. MSDS sheets for some materials in the lab may not be available here; in that case, refer to the hard copy MSDS book located over the acid cabinet in the cleanroom. This database is for Dartmouth use only.
