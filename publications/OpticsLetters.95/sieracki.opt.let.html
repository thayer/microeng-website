Accepted for publication in Optics Letters (May 15 1995 issue):

Simple Binary Optical Elements for Aberration Correction in Confocal Microscopy

Christian K. Sieracki, Christopher G. Levey, and Eric W. Hansen,

Thayer School of Engineering, Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH 03755.

ABSTRACT When a confocal fluorescence microscope with a high numerical aperture oil immersion objective is focused deep into an aqueous medium, aberrations result which weaken and blur the observed image. We have designed and fabricated a simple, two-level binary phase mask which partially corrects these aberrations, improving image brightness and resolution. The mask design and some confirming results are presented in this letter.
